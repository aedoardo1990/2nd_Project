Pole Position


A F1 memory card game to start in Pole Position!


How to be the first starting on the grid?

Uncover the matching pairs of F1 teammates. The fastest you match them and the higher chances you will have to start in pole ahead of everyone and of Super Max. Drivers will have different faces and you will recognize the matching teammates by the same colour of their racing suits.


You will be able to qualify in the following positions by stopping the timer accordingly:

  • 1st Time equal or less than 20s
  • 2nd Time equal or less than 30s
  • 3rd Time equal or less than 40s

If you don't know the Formula1 drivers and its teams, you can have an in-depth look on the official site of Formula1.
